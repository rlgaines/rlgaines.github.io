Portfolio

Emotion Analysis

Emotion Analysis is an app that allows companies or individuals to record a viewer and get a graphed data visualization of what they were feeling in real time. Anyone can record themselves and have an immediate response of what emotions they were expressing at that time. It has pages for uploading any video for custom results, an advertising page for companies, and a practice page for anyone who wants to practice their public speaking, acting, or interview skills.

< Tools: AngularJS, Node, Express, Socket.io, Kairos Emotion Analysis API, Add Pipe Video API, High-charts >

Imprints

Imprints is a hyperlocal guest book application that allows you to create a new guest book at any location that may be of interest to you. Other users can see where you have gone and written your imprint at, but they cannot write in that guest book unless they are exactly within geolocation of the guest book. This allows users to experience honest impressions of new places and entice them to go explore new guest books.

< Tools: Facebook Auth, Google Maps API, Node, Express, ProstreSql, KnexJS, Heroku >

Flashcards Study App

The Flashcard Study App will allow you to create an account. Once you are logged in you have the option of creating your own set of flashcards to study. Also, you have access to all of the other users flashcards in the "recommended decks" directory. This way you may study a deck of flashcards in another subject without creating it. All you have to do is add it to your own deck by clicking.

< Tools: AngularJS, PostgreSql, KnexJS, Node, Express, Auth, Heroku >

Not So Fifa

Not So Fifa is a two player soccer game. You and your opponent will have a set of keys that will control your character to move around the field. When manipulating the ball try to chase it down and kick it into the opponents goal. There is collision detection on the field and players allowing the ball to be in play at all times unless a player scores.

< Tools: HTML5 Canvas, jQuery, CSS3, JavaScript, Firebase >
